== Description ==

Listencat is a read aloud text to speech tool for audience growth and engagement that lets bloggers and publishers convert all your articles into natural, human sounding speech in minutes. The tool lets you automatically convert text to speech and integrate in your articles for seamless read aloud.

The text to speech plugin makes it easy for any blog - big or small - to engage and grow the traffic, improve the SEO ranking, attract more visitors and subscribers and ultimately monetize the blog.

Text to speech read aloud audio articles increase the time spent on your website by more than five times and thus improve your SEO ranking, which attracts more visitors and subscribers.

**HOW TO GET STARTED**

1) Install and activate the plugin.
2) Go to Listencat to sign up for FREE
3) Copy the authentification code from your Listencat.com account settings section
4) Go to your website WordPress settings and paste the code to unlock full features of Listencat
5) Turn your articles into natural sounding speech and let your audience listen to your content on the go.
